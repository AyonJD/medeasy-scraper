Usage Instructions Of Freedom Heavy Flow Wings 16 Pads:

  • Choose the right size and absorbency for your flow.
  • Apply the Freedom Heavy Flow Wings 16 Pads to your underwear, with the adhesive side down.
  • Secure the Freedom Heavy Flow Wings 16 Pads in place by folding the wings down.
